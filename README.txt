A WordPress plugin to adds Jifiti payment method to WooCommerce store.

== Description ==
[Jifiti’s](https://www.jifiti.com/) white-labeled Buy Now Pay Later platform facilitates end-to-end BNPL solutions, empowering retailers to convert more browsers into buyers and increase their purchasing power. 
Plug in the Jifiti BNPL for WooCommerce and we’ll connect you with a leading bank or lender. Through our BNPL platform, you can offer the full suite of consumer finance options, including installment payments, line of credit, 0% APR split payments, Pay in 4 and Pay in 3. 
White-label financing means that the BNPL is completely in your brand and your customer journey, with no detours or distractions. You own the customer experience from start to finish. 
By connecting you with the world’s leading banks and lenders, such as Citizens Pay, Mastercard, CaixaBank and Credit Agricole, you get access to the most regulated, competitive point-of-sale financing programs available.
Through our multi-lender financing platform, you are able to provide higher BNPL acceptance rates to your customers. Tier-1 lender didn’t approve? No problem! Our waterfall method automatically passes your customer’s financing application to the next lender in our network.
Hook up your store to digital payment services proven to increase sales by up to 40% and AOV by up to 70%!

== Features ==

1. High customer acceptance rates through multi-lender platform
2. Competitive bank-rate transaction fees 
3. Access to regulation-compliant BNPL programs
4. One easy integration to our BNPL platform
5. Full suite of BNPL options (installment loans, line of credit, interest-free split pay and more)
6. Seamless customer experience in your brand - no third-party detours!
7. Global solution in any international market
8. Full data transparency through reporting dashboard
9. Every channel (online, instore, call center), every checkout option

== Installation and Use ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

- Upload the plugin files to the directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
- Activate the plugin through the ‘Plugins’ screen in WordPress
- To setup the payment method go to WooCommerce > Settings > Payments > Cred payment > Set Up
- To setup the Promotional Messages go to Jifiti Offer By Price Widget
